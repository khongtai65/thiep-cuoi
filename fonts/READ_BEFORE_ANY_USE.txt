Holstein Script is a graceful calligraphic typeface where elegance meets playfulness. Its sweeping curves and ornate loops create a sense of charm while maintaining a refined structure. The tall ascenders and generous swashes give it a celebratory personality, yet it remains legible and balanced.  

With every letter thoughtfully crafted, it blends classic script traditions with a touch of modern flourish. Its decorative capitals draw the eye instantly, while the smooth lowercase forms carry the writing forward with rhythm and ease.  

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures and more automatic and manual features; all to give you full control and customizability. 
It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from North Europa to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers. 

Designed by Mans Greback, whose work often merges artistry with precision, Holstein Script showcases his commitment to creating letterforms that are as expressive as they are versatile.
